# PRD: Shunya Marketing Services Website ## 1. Project Overview **Brand Name:** Shunya Marketing Services **Positioning:** A complete business growth ecosystem offering Marketing, Technology, AI Automation, and Content Production. "From Zero to Scale — Shunya to Success." **Goal:** Build a premium, multi-page, high-converting website that feels like a production-level SaaS agency platform. ## 2. Design Direction - **Theme:** Dark mode with neon gradients (purple, blue, cyan glow) but with a **White or Warm White background** as requested in the final note (I will balance the dark "glassmorphism" elements against a clean, premium warm white backdrop for a unique 'Light-Futuristic' aesthetic). - **Style:** Ultra-modern, futuristic, Glassmorphism, minimal, Apple-level UI. - **Interactivity:** Smooth animations, micro-interactions, scroll-triggered effects, and transitions. ## 3. Sitemap & Page Requirements ### 1. Home Page - **Hero:** High-conversion headline + CTA. - **Trust Signals:** "2000+ leads/month" counter. - **Services Overview:** 5 core categories. - **Process:** "How We Scale" section. - **Social Proof:** Case studies preview and testimonials. - **Floating Elements:** AI chatbot popup + WhatsApp button. ### 2. Services Page - **Detailed sections for:** Performance Marketing, Tech Development, AI & Automation, Content Production, and Offline Marketing. - **Content:** Explanations, ROI/Scalability benefits, Use cases. - **Interactive:** Hover animations on service cards. ### 3. Portfolio Page - **Categories:** Websites, Ads, Video production, Case studies. - **Metrics:** Before/After results (ROAS, leads). - **Features:** Filter system and video preview cards. ### 4. About Page - **Story:** Shunya → Growth concept. - **Tone:** Founder-style authority, confident, punchy. - **USP:** Systems over services, AI-driven, full-stack. - **Visuals:** Timeline or journey animation. ### 5. Contact Page - **Conversion:** Lead form (Name, Phone, Business type). - **Integrations:** WhatsApp, Email/Phone display, Google Map placeholder. ### 6. Privacy Policy - Standard legal layout, clean formatting. ## 4. Key Features & UX - **Global Elements:** Sticky glassmorphism Navbar, comprehensive Footer. - **Conversion:** Sticky "Book Call" CTA, Lead magnets (Free Strategy PDF), Scarcity messaging. - **UX:** Page transitions, active nav highlighting, loading animations, smooth scrolling. ## 5. Copy & Messaging - **Style:** Hinglish + Simple English. - **Tone:** Direct, benefit-driven, "Marketing + Tech + AI = Complete Business Growth."
